Tips and Guides

Set screen-time limits that fit your routine

Make a clear plan for when you’ll use your phone, laptop, or games—like after homework or for a fixed amount of time. This helps you stay focused and avoid scrolling for hours.

Use technology for learning before entertainment

Try to finish school tasks, research, or creative projects first. When you use tech with a purpose, it feels more productive and reduces stress.

Protect your digital well-being

Keep notifications limited, avoid using devices late at night, and take short breaks during long screen use. This helps your eyes, your sleep, and your mental focus.
